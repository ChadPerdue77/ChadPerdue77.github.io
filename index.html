About Me

I’m Chad Perdue, a current Junior at Beech Grove High School. I’m mostly like an introvert and typically can be seen with headphones in and trying to decide what I want to listen to next. A music addict is probably the best way to describe me, because I’m always trying to find new music in the wide span of genres I enjoy. The key to my lifestyle is the idea of growth, I believe that you have to be uncomfortable at some point in order to grow. I believe that mistakes are just learning experiences and as long as I grow from my mistakes, I can never fail. I don’t have many huge interests and generally I’m unsure of what my interests are. I enjoy problem solving in most situations, even if my role is just pondering on solutions to myself. My peers would likely describe me as easy-going, laid-back, chill, and funny. I’ve found myself to be a hard-worker most of the time and especially in a group work setting. In my life, my largest influences are from people that I’ve never met before. I’m one to find inspiration in nearly anything, and my outlook on life changes nearly everyday.

I would like to thank my mother for not only creating me but also for demonstrating what it means to be a parent, an independent adult, and to have values.
